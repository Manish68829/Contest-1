The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearane to much larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting here, known as beagling. Processing a geat sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in qurantine aroud the world. The beagle is intelligent and is popular pet due to its size, good temper and lack on inherited health problems.
